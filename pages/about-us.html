About Us

Business Minds with Technology Solutions 

We know that technology is a critical component to your organization’s success. It is the way you operate your organization, communicate with internal and external partners, and fulfill everyday functions to keep your company running. We also know that when the right technology is leveraged, integrated and customized – that is when employees are empowered, processes are streamlined and greater success is achieved.

Understanding what modern technologies are available for your organization and how they fit within your organization’s needs can be a powerful thing.

That is where we step in. We are a small firm by design with only senior resources. In fact, our average tenure in IT is 21 years. With this expertise and experience, we fundamentally transform businesses to accelerate growth, decrease costs and improve performance. Our consultants identify critical value levers and drive growth by tightly linking business consulting with innovative, custom technology solutions across the following core areas of service:

  • Envisioning
  • Custom Application Development
  • Intranet/Extranet Development & Design
  • Business Intelligence
