APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

Make Your Business Process Personal

Acrowire’s proven, experienced software application development team researches, engineers and deploys custom applications to meet your business objectives. Whether you need custom reporting, business intelligence, automated feeds or multiple systems to be integrated – we can help. Our Six Sigma-based methodologies combined with our technical expertise and drive for business results will help your organization become more competitive, profitable and productive.

  

We specialize in three core areas within our Application Development Practice:

Application Modernization

Rewrite legacy code with modern development technologies to:

  • Leverage the latest capabilities offered by new technologies
  • More easily find resources familiar with underlying technologies
  • Retire infrastructure associated with legacy platforms

Greenfield Custom Application Development

Build an application from scratch (desktop or mobile) that is unique to your organization, and:

  • Create functionality that aligns exactly with your company’s needs/objectives
  • Have the ability to add capabilities as new needs arise
  • Reduce reliance on product vendors appetite to innovate

Systems Integration

Allow two applications to interact with one another and automate feeds between platforms to:

  • Eliminate duplicate data entries and processes
  • Ensure data feeds are always up-to-date
  • Optimize productivity

Application MD

A comprehensive health check for your existing applications. Don’t let business critical applications be a ticking time bomb. Ask us about Application MD today.
