ILTA SPS: Taking SharePoint to the Cloud – Presentation Now Available

Posted on June 14, 2015 by Ted Theodoropoulos

Earlier last week, I had the honor and pleasure of speaking with Guy Wiggins (Kelley Drye & Warren) and Kara Portwood (Armstrong Teasdale) at ILTA SharePoint Symposium. Our session was titled: All You Need to Know to Take SharePoint to the Cloud.

How can organizations leverage the Microsoft Cloud effectively while taking advantage of what they’ve already invested in their SharePoint implementations? What are the real business scenarios in which you would use/implement SharePoint in the cloud? What are the biggest  challenges to utilizing the cloud, and how do they tie to SharePoint? How do you decide on one cloud provider versus another? Learn the answers to these questions and more as experts share knowledge gained from implementing SharePoint in the cloud.

ILTA has posted the presentation of this session if you were unable to attend.

To learn more about SharePoint Online and how it compares to on-prem (pros and cons, feature parity, security, third party integration, costs, etc.), you can also learn more by attending one of our ILTA roadshows.

Thank you to everyone who came out to our booth to learn more about our partnership with BA Insight and our SharePoint capabilities. We had a great time meeting new people and catching up with old friends.
