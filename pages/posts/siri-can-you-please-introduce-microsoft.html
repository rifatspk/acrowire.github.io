“Siri, Can you please introduce Microsoft?”

Posted on May 5, 2016 by Chelsea Theodoropoulos

The next time you ask Siri to “Call Mom,” the elevation of Mount Kilimanjaro, or if she loves you back, put this face to the iconic iPhone voice:

Just as I was able to yesterday at Charlotte’s Digital Summit, meet Susan Bennett. She’s warm, full of smiles and just as entertaining as Siri.

Since being unveiled in 2013 as Siri, a personal assistant to over 100 million users daily, she has been gaining fame and media focus globally. Outside of Siri, Susan’s voice has been used in a number of commercials and productions with clients such as: AT&T, Ford, the Discovery Channel, Coca-Cola, Fisher Price, McDonald’s, The Home Depot, Goodyear, VISA, Macy’s, Club Med, Delta Airlines, and The Cartoon Network.

Not only was I fortunate enough to shake her hand, but I was able to witness two tech giants coming together as she introduced Microsoft Behavioral Scientist, Matt Wallaert, on stage for the keynote.

And now, you can also.
