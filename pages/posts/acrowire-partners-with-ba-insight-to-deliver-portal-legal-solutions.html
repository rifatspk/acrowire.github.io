Acrowire Partners with BA Insight to Deliver Portal Legal Solutions

Posted on June 4, 2015 by Ted Theodoropoulos

Today, we announce our partnership with BA Insight to deliver joint technology solutions to the legal market. BA Insight is a software provider that accelerates and future-proofs the creation of SharePoint portals and delivers content connectivity/classification software and user experience applications. With our SharePoint development and design expertise and legal experience and BA Insight’s extensive legal and innovative software portfolio, we are proud to offer our clients a comprehensive service and software portal solution that now meet the needs of the evolving industry. The partnership will provide law firms flexible, custom solutions with unparalleled access to information (structured, unstructured, on-premise, cloud) all while leveraging the existing or future SharePoint investment.

“This partnership is built upon the core competencies of two insightful, passionate organizations recognized for their expertise in the development and application of technology that helps our mutual legal customers take advantage of their collective knowledge, greatly improving productivity. Together we provide the broadest and deepest set of solutions to the legal industry.” – BA Insight, CEO and Chairman, Massood Zarrabian

BA Insight’s legal software portfolio includes: Matter Comparison, User-Generated InfoSites, Enhanced People Search, Auto-Classification, Smart Previews and secure connectivity to practice management systems including Aderant, Worksite/iManage, Elite, and PLC/PracticalLaw, and dramatically reduces the time, cost, effort, and risk of implementing SharePoint portals while providing a greatly improved user experience. BA Insight serves legal entities such as: DLA Piper, Tory’s, Wiley Rein, Travers Smith, Dorsey & Whitney, Kelley Drye, Fenwick & West, and Lewis Silkin.

You can learn more about our partnership with BA Insight by contacting us here, or visiting our booth (#33) at the ILTA SharePoint Symposium next week to meet both teams and preview portal software solutions.
